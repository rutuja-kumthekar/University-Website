About Us

Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU) is a unitary, Maharashtra state Technological University in Lonere, Maharashtra, India.
This is the only govt autonomous university in Maharashtra. The college has a very nice infrastructure, very well managed and the nice collection of books in library, well-equipped laboratories and a huge playground.
